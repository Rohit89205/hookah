FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you provide the Alcohol?

No. Liquor laws prevent us from providing the alcohol for your event. All liquor and beer must be provided by the host.

Is gratuity included?

Tips are customary in the bartending business so we offer our customers two options:

  • Allow a tip jar to be placed on the bar for guests.
  • "Hosted Gratuity" There will be no tip jar and the host will pay a 20% gratuity fee based on number of guest attending the event.

How will the bartenders be dressed?

Your Party bartender (s) will be dressed according to the type of event you are having in proper attire. Special requests can be made. We will discuss this with you at the time the event is booked.

Who is responsible for making sure that guests do not have too much to drink?

Our bartenders are trained in alcohol awareness. However, the host of the event is ultimately responsible for all invited guests and their actions. Party bartender reserves the right to refuse service to any guest(s) who seem to have had too much to drink, per our judgment.

How do I reserve my event date with Party Bartender Services?

A Non-Refundable 50% deposit is required at the time of the contract signing to reserve the date and time of your special event. The Non-Refundable retainer may be paid via Cash or PayPal.

What type of payment do you accept?

Types of payment accepted are Cash and Credit Cards. Credit Card payments may be made in advance securely through PayPal. Full Payment or any Balance owed is expected to be received one week prior to the event. Staff will not perform any duties until your full payment is received.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation Policy: Within 3 days 50% refund of charges less the non-refundable deposit. No refund within 48 hrs of event. Cancellation must be submitted via email
